Label Printing


You most likely do not need to change these settings!
  1. Print your labels on regular paper
  2. Cut them out with scissors
  3. Use packing tape to attach them to your lots (make sure your bags are dry!)

If you want to print on self-adhesive labels, search for small labels or large labels, and make sure to print using a laser jet printer (inkjet runs when it's wet).
